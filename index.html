How to Use an Instagram Photo Downloader

Using an Instagram photo downloader is usually a straightforward process. Follow these simple steps to start saving your favorite photos:

  1. Open the Instagram app or website and navigate to the photo you want to download.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu on the post and select "Copy Link."
  3. Open your chosen Instagram photo downloader in a web browser or app.
  4. Paste the copied link into the designated input field of the downloader.
  5. Click the download button, and the image will be saved to your device.
